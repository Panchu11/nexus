IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

✅ Filenames are CASE-SENSITIVE on Mac/Linux
✅ Use .jpg or .png format (not .jpeg)
✅ Logo can be .png or .svg
✅ All images should be 500x500px minimum
✅ Square aspect ratio (1:1) works best
✅ Compress images to < 500KB each

❌ Don't use:
   - Different filenames
   - Different folder locations
   - Different image formats
   - Uppercase letters in filenames
